Welcome to Eco tour camp - Jogad

DEVJIBHAI'S Kooba Huts (Home Stay)

Welcome to our eco-friendly 'Kooba' in the Little Rann of Kutch (LRK)

We are located just across the street from the unique Little Rann desert and World's only Wild Ass Sanctuary

In our humble abode, we offer local vegetarian cuisine, ecologically responsible wildlife safari,

a chance to reconnect with nature and a sky full of stars. Welcome home!

Situated in the western state of Gujarat, this is a part of country yet to be discovered by tourists and a quiet haven for nature and wildlife.

  • Each kooba is designed to stay cool hygienic in the soaring desert temperatures and comes with en-suite toilet and bucket bath.
  • Our guests say that local, home cooked food prepared in our kitchen is delicious and fullfilling !
  • The best time to visit the LRK is from October to March when the monsoon has subsided.The Wild Ass Sanctuary is in fact, closed to visitors between 15th June to 15th October during rainy season

The best way to get to Eco Tour Camp is from the nearby town DHRANGADHRA / HALVAD which is well connected by train and bus to the capital city Ahemdabad in Gujarat,INDIA
